My name is Patrick and I was born 1983 in south Germany. I live there for over 40 years and got infected with the HF-virus by the age of 10. I started with CB-Radio in 1993 but due to lack of a good antenna unfortunately I got almost no DX contacts.

After years of silence my friend Michael (DO1WMA) brought me back ON THE Air again and so 2023 I got my HAM license.
